* Platform Configuration: ASA A30 Single Chassis HA Pair
* Max Raw Capacity: 1.1016 PB
* Memory: 128.0000 GB
* Form Factor: 2U chassis with 2 HA controllers and 24 drive slots
* ONTAP Version: ONTAP: 9.16.1P2
* PCIe Expansion Slots: 8
