= Replace a power supply - AFF A5X
:icons: font
:imagesdir: ../media/

[.lead]
Replace an AC or DC power supply unit (PSU) in your AFF A5X system when it fails or becomes faulty, ensuring that your system continues to receive the required power for stable operation. The replacement process involves disconnecting the faulty PSU from the power source, unplugging the power cable, replacing the faulty PSU, and then reconnecting it to the power source.
