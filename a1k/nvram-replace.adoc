= Replace NVRAM - AFF A1K
:icons: font
:imagesdir: ../media/

[.lead]
Replace the NVRAM in your AFF A1K system when the non-volatile memory becomes faulty or requires an upgrade. The replacement process involves
shutting down the impaired controller, replacing the NVRAM module or the NVRAM DIMM, reassigning the disks, and returning the failed part to NetApp.
