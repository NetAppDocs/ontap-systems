= Replace a DIMM - AFF A70 and AFF A90
:icons: font
:imagesdir: ../media/

[.lead]
Replace a DIMM in  your AFF A70 or AFF A90 system when excessive correctable or uncorrectable errors are detected. These issues, often caused by a single DIMM failure, can prevent the storage system from booting ONTAP.
