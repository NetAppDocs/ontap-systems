= Replace a DIMM - AFF A70 and AFF A90
:icons: font
:imagesdir: ../media/

[.lead]
Replace a DIMM in your AFF A70 or AFF A90 system if excessive correctable or uncorrectable errors are detected. Such errors can prevent the storage system from booting ONTAP. The replacement process involves shutting down the impaired controller, removing it, replacing the DIMM, and then reinstalling the controller.
