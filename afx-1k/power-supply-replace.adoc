= Replace a power supply - AFX 1K
:icons: font
:imagesdir: ../media/

[.lead]
Replace an AC or DC power supply unit (PSU) in your AFX 1K storage system when it fails or becomes faulty, ensuring that your system continues to receive the required power for stable operation. The replacement process involves disconnecting the target PSU, unplugging the power cable, removing the faulty PSU and installing the replacement PSU, and then reconnecting it to the power source.
