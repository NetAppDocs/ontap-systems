= Replace a fan - AFX 1K
:icons: font
:imagesdir: ../media/

[.lead]
Replace a fan module in your AFX 1K storage system when a fan fails or is not operating efficiently, as this can affect system cooling and overall performance. The replacement process involves removing the controller, reinstalling the controller, and returning the failed part to NetApp.
