= Replace a power supply - AFF C80
:icons: font
:imagesdir: ../media/

[.lead]
Replace an AC or DC power supply unit (PSU) in your AFF C80 system when it fails or becomes faulty, ensuring that your system continues to receive the required power for stable operation. The replacement process involves disconnecting the target PSU from the power source, unplugging the power cable, removing the faulty PSU and installing the replacement PSU, and then reconnecting it to the power source.
