= Requirement to replace the chassis - ASA A70 and ASA A90
:icons: font

Before replacing the chassis of your ASA A70 or ASA A90 system, ensure you meet the necessary requirements for a successful replacement. This includes verifying all other components in the system are functioning properly, verifying that you have local administrator credentials for ONTAP, the correct replacement chassis, and the necessary tools.
