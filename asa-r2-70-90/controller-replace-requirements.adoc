= Controller replace requirements - ASA A70 and ASA A90
:icons: font
:imagesdir: ../media/

[.lead]
Before replacing the controller in your ASA A70 or ASA A90 system, ensure you meet the necessary requirements for a successful replacement. This includes having the correct replacement controller, a backup of your system configuration, and access to any required tools and firmware.
