= Shut down the controller - ASA A20, ASA A30, and ASA A50
:icons: font
:imagesdir: ../media/

[.lead]
Shut down the controller in your ASA A20, ASA A30, or ASA A50 storage system when when you need to replace the boot media. Before performing maintenance or component replacement it is critical to shut down the controller safely to prevent data loss and ensure system stability.
