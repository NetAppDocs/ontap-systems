= Replace a fan module - ASA A20, ASA A30, and ASA A50
:icons: font
:imagesdir: ../media/

[.lead]
Replace a fan module in your AFF A20, AFF A30, or AFF A50 system when a fan fails or is not operating efficiently, as this can affect system cooling and overall performance. The replacement process involves shutting down the controller, removing the controller, replacing the fan, reinstalling the controller, and returning the failed part to NetApp.
