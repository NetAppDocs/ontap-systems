= Power on and configure the switches for your AFX 1K storage system
:icons: font
:imagesdir: ../media/

[.lead]
After you cable your AFX 1K storage system, you need to power on and configure the switches. 

.Steps
. Plug the power cords for the switches into the power sources. 
. Power on each switch. 
. Configure the switches to support the AFX 1K storage system.
* For Cisco Nexus 9332D-GX2B switches, see link:https://docs.netapp.com/us-en/ontap-systems-switches/switch-cisco-9332d-gx2b/setup-switch-9332d-cluster.html[Configure Cisco Nexus 9332D-GX2B switch^].
* For Cisco Nexus 9364D-GX2A switches, see link:https://docs.netapp.com/us-en/ontap-systems-switches/switch-cisco-9364d-gx2a/setup-switch-9364d-cluster.html[Configure Cisco Nexus 9364D-GX2A switch^].
