= Replace the chassis - FAS70 and FAS90
:icons: font
:imagesdir: ../media/

[.lead]
Replace the chassis of your FAS70 and FAS90 system when a hardware failure requires it. The replacement process involves removing the controller from the front of the chassis, and removing the power supply units (PSUs), NVRAM12, I/O modules and blanks, and system management module and then installing the replacement chassis, and reinstalling the chassis components.
