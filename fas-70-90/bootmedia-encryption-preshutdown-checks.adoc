= Check encryption key support and status on the boot media - FAS70 and FAS90
:icons: font
:imagesdir: ../media/

[.lead]
To ensure data security on your FAS70 or FAS90 storage system, you need to verify the encryption key support and status on your boot media. Check if your ONTAP version supports NetApp Volume Encryption (NVE), and before you shut down the controller check if the key manager is active.

include::../_include/encryption_check_bootmedia.adoc[]

.What's next?
After checking the encryption key support and status on the boot media, you need to link:bootmedia-shutdown.html[shut down the controller].
