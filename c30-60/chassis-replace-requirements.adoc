= Requirements to replace the chassis - AFF C30 and AFF C60
:icons: font
:imagesdir: ../media/

[.lead]
Before replacing the chassis in your AFF A20, AFF A30, or AFF A50 system, ensure you meet the necessary requirements for a successful replacement. This includes verifying all other components in the system are functioning properly, verifying that you have the correct replacement chassis, and the necessary tools.
