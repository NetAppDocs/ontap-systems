= Shut down the controllers - AFF C30 and AFF C60
:icons: font
:imagesdir: ../media/

[.lead]
Shut down the controllers in your AFF A20, AFF A30, or AFF A50 storage system to prevent data loss and ensure system stability when replacing the chassis.
