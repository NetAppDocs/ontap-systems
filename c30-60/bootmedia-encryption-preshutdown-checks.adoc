= Check encryption key support and status on the boot media - AFF C30 and AFF C60
:icons: font
:imagesdir: ../media/

[.lead]
To ensure data security on your AFF C30 or AFF C60 storage system, you need to verify the encryption key support and status on your boot media. Check if your ONTAP version supports NetApp Volume Encryption (NVE), and before you shut down the controller check if the key manager is active. 

include::../_include/encryption_check_bootmedia.adoc[]

.What's next?
After checking the encryption key support and status on the boot media, you need to link:bootmedia-shutdown.html[shut down the controller].
